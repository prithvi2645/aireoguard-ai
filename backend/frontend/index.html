What is AeroGuard AI?

AeroGuard AI is an advanced AI-powered predictive maintenance platform designed specifically for aviation jet engines. By analyzing real-time sensor data from 21 critical engine parameters, our machine learning model predicts the Remaining Useful Life (RUL) of engine components — enabling maintenance teams to act before failures happen.

Built on the NASA C-MAPSS turbofan engine degradation dataset, AeroGuard AI brings cutting-edge deep learning into the hands of aviation engineers and fleet managers.

The Problem

Unplanned Failures

Unexpected jet engine failures cost airlines $3.5 billion annually in delays, cancellations, and emergency repairs — putting passengers and crew at risk.

Wasteful Scheduled Maintenance

Traditional time-based maintenance replaces parts that still have useful life, wasting up to 30% of maintenance budgets on unnecessary overhauls.

No Real-Time Insight

Current systems lack the ability to process live sensor streams and provide actionable predictions — leaving engineers reactive instead of proactive.
